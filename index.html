❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about THE VOID bot.

How do I connect my WhatsApp to THE VOID? +
Simply visit our connect page, enter your phone number in the format 1305xxxx, and you'll receive a pairing code. Open WhatsApp > Settings > Linked Devices > Link a Device, then enter the provided code.
Is THE VOID safe to use? +
Absolutely! THE VOID uses secure MongoDB storage, encrypted sessions, and follows WhatsApp's official API guidelines. Your data is protected with enterprise-level security measures.
Can I use multiple WhatsApp numbers? +
Yes! THE VOID supports multi-user functionality, allowing you to connect multiple WhatsApp numbers simultaneously with independent session management.
What happens if I lose connection? +
THE VOID features automatic reconnection and session recovery. If you lose connection, the bot will automatically attempt to restore your session without requiring re-pairing.
How do I delete my session? +
You can delete your session anytime by sending .deleteme command. This will permanently remove all your data and disconnect the bot from your WhatsApp account.
Are there any usage limits? +
THE VOID is designed for fair usage. While there are no strict limits, excessive usage may be throttled to ensure optimal performance for all users.
